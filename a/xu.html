Thanks to AI’s far-sighted reschedule, we had a GREAT day at Ping Yao! Once as a fortress and commercial center, Ping Yao was famous for its historical sites. There is the first draft bank in China, Rishengchang evolving from a dye-house (someone comprehended draft bank as the preliminary version of bank, but actually draft means an order for the payment of money here…Xu could be smart occasionally~). To avoid cronyism and corruption, all shareholders of Rishengchang had no family ties (they had disparate last name). On the contrary, the escort agency in the neighbor were composed of close relatives and good friends, because 走镖 is such a dangerous task that concerted efforts and selfless devotion were essential for the accomplishment of mission and their own survivals. Some missions even required escorts(镖师) to go abroad and take several years to complete. We heard that these chivalrous men once went to Russia to rescue a hostage. This is really incredible at that time! Just like AI said, escort agency is to draft bank what cash carrier to bank today, escort agency and draft bank were interdependent, and I guess both of them played a crucial role on realizing “汇通天下”. What impressed me most at Ping Yao is definitely AI’s fascinating story about Dragon’s Nine Sons “龙生九子”！Where there is AI, there is endless fun!
